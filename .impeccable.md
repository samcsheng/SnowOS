## Design Context

### Users
Three distinct roles — **Instructors** (ski professionals managing their daily schedules and writing lesson reports on mobile), **Supervisors** (operations managers overseeing the full day's lessons on tablet/desktop), and **Guests** (resort visitors booking lessons and tracking their ski progress on mobile). All are on-property at a luxury ski resort (Tomamu, Japan). The interface is used in quick, task-focused bursts — between lessons, at the ski school desk, or while waiting at the lifts.

### Brand Personality
**Warm. Precise. Luxury.**

Club Med's Trident brand communicates premium hospitality with confidence. It is never cold, minimalist-for-minimalism's-sake, or corporate-grey. The saffron yellow is the hero — it brings warmth and energy. Black Coal grounds everything with authority. Together they create an interface that feels like it belongs at a luxury mountain resort.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Primary action color**: Saffron Yellow `#FDBE00` — CTAs, active states, header, selected filters
- **Grounding color**: Black Coal `#000000` — headings, dark buttons, selected tabs, primary text
- **Surfaces**: Cloud White `#FFFFFF` cards on Pearl `#F8F8F8` background
- **Typography**: Newsreader Bold for headings (serif warmth), Inter for UI text (clean precision)
- **Shape language**: Full pill (`border-radius: 9999px`) for all interactive elements (buttons, badges, tabs, filters); `border-radius: 8–12px` for cards and containers
- **Reference**: Club Med's own Trident design system — warm, confident, luxury travel
- **Anti-reference**: Generic SaaS grey, cold brutalist minimal, anything that reads as "enterprise"

### Design Principles
1. **Saffron leads action** — Every primary CTA is saffron yellow with black text. Never place light text on saffron.
2. **Pill shapes everywhere** — Buttons, badges, tabs, filters, inputs all use full pill rounding. Cards use medium rounding (8–12px). Mixing border-radius styles is a violation.
3. **Semantic color = function only** — Green (#088A20), Red (#BF2F17), Orange (#C75300) are exclusively for validation states and status feedback. Never use them for decoration.
4. **4px grid discipline** — All spacing, font sizes, and border radius values must be multiples of 4.
5. **Pair color with icon** — Never rely on color alone to convey meaning. Valid states show ✓, errors show ✗, warnings show ⚠.
